Tamojit Chakraborty

A Marine Engineer by profession, Tamojit Chakraborty comes from a family of Purohits and Vedic Scholars in Bengal. Continually exposed to various aspects of religion and spirituality since a very young age, his quest for solutions to the various problems faced by mankind led him to the renowned Mahakaleshwar Jyotirling in Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh, where with the divine blessings of Shri Mahakal, his Ishta Deva, he plunged into an extensive study and research of various scriptures, pujas, stotras, havans, ceremonies and their efficacy in solving the problems of our fellow human beings. His analytical approach to the world of Hindu religion and spirituality, clarity in understanding problems of worldly life and decisiveness in prescribing appropriate solutions have benefitted many people over the years.

In the course of his travels to various holy places all over India, Tamojit has forged professional alliances with many Pandits, some of whom comprise his Team of Experts featured on this website and are always at your service. He believes in serving mankind not for any material gains, but as a means to contribute to the welfare of the society and express his gratitude to the Almighty whose blessings have rewarded him with the dual wealth of knowledge and compassion. This website is intended to serve as a platform in reaching out to those who desire to tap into the immense and vast ocean of Hinduism in order to tide over their difficulties in the material world.

We welcome you to this website and hope you avail of the manifold resources of Tamojit and his multi-dimensional Team of Experts.
